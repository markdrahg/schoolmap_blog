What is School Map?

June 6, 2024 by Mark

The School Map project is a prototype for a forthcoming web application. Its objective is to assist users by providing a list of suitable universities that align with their educational requirements.

What is the purpose of Schol Map?

The School Map project is an innovative initiative designed to consolidate information on hundreds of universities onto a single platform. This reduces the complexity and stress for users typically associated with searching the web, facilitating the identification of the most suitable and optimal universities.

Our story

As part of an online software engineering assignment from ALX SE, my partner and I were tasked with developing a project.

Reflecting on previous challenges, I recalled the difficulty I faced in researching universities that matched my personality and academic background. This experience inspired me to create an innovative project, "School Map." The project's goal is to assist individuals worldwide by providing a platform that suggests hundreds of universities based on their requirements, desired programs, and current educational status, qualifications, and background.

I shared the concept with my partner, and she agreed to work on developing a prototype for our assignment. However, upon further reflection, I realized the potential impact of this project and decided it should extend beyond a mere prototype submission.

During a meeting, we presented our prototype to senior developers, who supported the idea and encouraged us to pursue its full development.

From the initial idea development to showcasing the prototype, I gained valuable experience and resolved to bring School Map to fruition, even if my partner chose not to continue with the project.

Stay tuned for the launch of School Map, coming soon.
